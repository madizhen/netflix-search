What to watch?

The problem with giant libraries is that there can be just too much stuff. It takes time and effort to go and look for something, when you could be spending that precious time consuming whatever product you've been trying to pick from already. This site will simply pick shows for you watch on Netflix. You can use the Pick a Movie Button for grab 'n' go or you can filter with the genre--and nothing else that might make you second guess the selection.
